◆ e_render_request

enum e_render_request

Base for typedef t_render_request.

This type is used in function fdf_render to indicate what is needed to re-calculate and redraw.

Enumerator
FROM_WORLD 

The re-calculations must begin at transformations from model to WORLD space.

FROM_CAMERA 

The re-calculations must begin at transformations from world to CAMERA space.

FROM_SCREEN 

The re-calculations must begin at transformations from camera to SCREEN view space.

FROM_IMAGE 

The re-calculations must begin at IMAGE (pixel-based) level.

NO_RENDER 

There is no need to render.
